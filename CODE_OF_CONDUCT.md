# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

ECT is a research framework built on intellectual honesty, rigor, and curiosity. Everyone who contributes or engages is expected to uphold these principles.

### We expect:
- Good faith engagement with ideas, even radical ones
- Honest attribution of prior work
- Clear labeling of what is proven vs conjectured vs speculative
- Respectful disagreement — challenge ideas, not people
- Openness to being wrong

### We do not tolerate:
- Intellectual dishonesty or plagiarism
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Bad faith engagement designed to derail research
- Gatekeeping based on credentials or institutional affiliation

## On Credentials

ECT was founded by a self-taught independent researcher. Institutional affiliation is irrelevant here. What matters is the quality of your reasoning and the honesty of your engagement.

If your argument is sound, it will be taken seriously regardless of who you are.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to divinesunday247@gmail.com. Persistent bad faith actors will be blocked from contributing.
